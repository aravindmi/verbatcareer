Primary Responsibilities:


The .Net Developer designs, modifies, develops, writes and implements software programming applications and components; supports and/or installs software applications and components; works from written specifications and pre-established guidelines to perform the functions of the job and maintains documentation of process flow.

Skill Requirements:


  • Utilize established development tools, guidelines and conventions including but not limited to ASP.NET, SQL Server, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery and C#.
  • Exposure to MVC Framework.
  • Enhance existing systems by analysing business objectives, preparing an action plan and identifying areas for modification and improvement.
  • Investigate and develop skills in new technologies.
  • Create technical specifications and test plans.
  • Candidates ideally would possess 3- 5 years of experience.
