Job Function:


We are looking for experienced UI Developer with strong knowledge in HTML5, CSS3, JQuery, JavaScript. Candidate should have good working knowledge in creating Responsive Design and should be a good visualizer. Must be able to work closely with the members of the development team to deliver high quality web applications.

Desired Profile:


  • More than 1.5 years of experience.
  • Collaborate cross-functionally to deliver great user experiences.
  • Requirements: Portfolio featuring comprehensive examples of product interfaces, web design, and creative branding work.
  • Qualification: Any Graduate with desired skills.

Preferred Skills:


  • Understanding of the capabilities and limitations of XHMTL, HTML5, javascript, CSS3 and JQuery.
  • Working experience with Bootstrap and Responsive Design.
  • Knowledge in SEO is an added advantage.
  • A self-learner, who can keep up with latest design paradigms.
  • A keen eye for good design.
  • Good communication skills to work with developers and testers.
