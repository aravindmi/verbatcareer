Job Function:


We are looking for Senior Presales Executives with experience in successfully structuring winning client propositions and enthusiasm for emerging trends in Digital Technologies. The candidates should possess a proven track record of capabilities in Requirement Scoping, Functional Analysis, Effort & Cost Estimation, Commercial Deal Structuring, Proposal Preparation and Client Presentation.

Must have:


  • Good communication skills and experience in regular interfacing with clients & onsite teams.
  • Proven ability to prepare and drive timely bid participation from required internal teams.
  • Ambitious and passionate to grow and do things differently.
  • Digital Technology admirer – keeping abreast of constantly changing technology environment.
  • Industry watcher – keeping abreast of latest competition and innovative commercial trends in the markets.

Ideal:


  • Experience of managing bids for US, European or Middle East clients and prospects.

Qualification:


  • BE/BTech/MBA with 3+ years of experience in pre-sales.
