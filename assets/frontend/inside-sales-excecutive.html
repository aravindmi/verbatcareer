Job Function:


We are looking for an Inside Sales Executive to identify and connect with UK-based IT Directors, IT Managers and senior Decision makers across various industries (SMEs to Large corporates), to pitch Verbat’s Digital Technology Services. Client interaction will primarily be through email and social media (LinkedIn, Twitter etc), with an element of phone conversations. This is a target driven role and does not involve field work or travel.

Primary Responsibilities:


  • Create new business opportunities for Verbat by lead creation (via Databases, LinkedIn and other Social media) and lead qualification (Emails and Calls).
  • Articulate Verbat’s offerings and engage in meaningful conversation/s with prospects.
  • Manage lead generation forecasts on a periodic basis and track customer data using company-standard MIS & CRM tools.
  • Collateral Support for Client pitches, Execute marketing activities such as email campaigns, call outs etc.
  • Meet assigned monthly lead generation quota.

Qualifications:


  • Graduate, preferably in IT / Computers. Undergraduates with 2+ years relevant experience will also be considered.
  • 1 to 3 years’ experience in Inside sales / Lead generation.
  • Technical solutions sales experience in Digital / IT services in a B2B environment.
  • Experience (or confident) of connecting with senior IT executives within all size organisations through emails, social media and calls.
  • Adept at doing secondary research on companies and individuals (key decision makers) using different data sources.
  • Strong oral and written English communication. Working knowledge of MS Office, especially Word and Excel.
  • High energy, self-motivated go getter with aspirations to grow fast up the ladder.
